About JapanLA
JapanLA is a retail shop that caters to adults who love “kawaii” or “cute” character goods. They carry toys, gifts, apparel, accessories, art, and more, all inspired by Japanese and Los Angeles Pop Culture. JapanLA is a lifestyle brand, creating licensed goods and collaborating with brands including Sanrio, Rilakkuma, Pokemon, Studio Ghibli, and more!
